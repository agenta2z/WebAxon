25+ Best Starbucks Chocolate Drinks To Try (Including Secret Menu Items)

17 min read MAY 12, 2023

Craving something sweet but don't know what? Look no further than the 25+ best Starbucks chocolate drinks. From traditional orders to secret menu items, there's something for everyone here. Put your sweet tooth in charge and try one of these delicious drinks. Unlock its hidden flavors with various sauces and whippings – or just keep it simple with classic chocolate-flavored coffee blends.

25 Best Chocolate Drinks To Order At Starbucks For the perfect chocolaty experience, definitely try out some of Starbucks' best offerings like the Mocha Cookie Crumble Frappuccino, Reserve Dark Chocolate Mocha, Peppermint White Hot Chocolate, and Ferrero Rocher Frappuccino. Caffe Mocha
The Caffe Mocha is a rich flavored espresso beverage available at Starbucks. This creamy treat combines the chocolate flavors of mocha sauce with freshly steamed milk and an added shot of espresso.Caffe Mochas can also be made sugar-free and non-fat if desired. Lactose-free substitutes like almond or coconut milk are also available as options. Some may choose to customize their Mocha by adding other syrups such as hazelnut, raspberry, vanilla, and more for a sweeter flavor profile tailored to their tastes.
Pairing the popular mocha frappuccino frappe with any type of dessert would make a delicious finale to any meal. There are ample options from brownies to truffles or angel food cake to pancakes-it really is up to you.

White Chocolate Mocha

The Starbucks White Chocolate Mocha is a delicious beverage that combines creamy white chocolate mocha sauce with espresso and is topped with light whipped cream. Treat yourself to this drink, as its rich ingredients are perfectly balanced to make this syrup-based beverage a decadent delight.

This popular Starbucks drink has been featured across several media outlets, such as Cosmopolitan and BuzzFeed. It has become one of their most beloved treats due to its wide variety of interpretations.

With both hot and iced versions available, customers can enjoy this delicious drink any time during the year. There are also many creative ways to customize your White Chocolate Mocha, starting from adding nut-based milk like almond instead of regular dairy milk or using flavored syrups from hazelnut to peppermint extract.

Iced White Chocolate Mocha

The Iced White Chocolate Mocha is a delicious treat for any chocolate lover. This sweet blend is made with Starbucks' signature white chocolate sauce and steamed milk, which offers a chocolaty experience that can be enjoyed hot or cold.Its star ingredient, White Chocolate Mocha Sauce, is made with condensed skimmed milk, sugar, cocoa butter, and coconut oil to create a creamy, rich flavor you won't soon forget.The Iced White Chocolate Mocha stands out from other chocolate drinks served at Starbucks as it adds cool base notes instead of piping hot espresso. It allows the flavor combinations to really stand out on their own.This makes it one of the most popular iced coffee offerings and one of the best-tasting beverages available from this prominent coffeehouse chain.

Mocha Cookie Crumble Frappuccino

The Mocha Cookie Crumble Frappuccino is a must-try treat for chocolate lovers. This creamy espresso drink combines mocha sauce, dark cocoa, and milk to create a delicious blend of flavors.This delicious beverage is topped with whipped cream, which also features cookie crumble pieces for an extra crunchy texture.For those who prefer their coffee a little sweeter, adding Starbucks' classic syrup can take it up a notch. If you are craving a specific taste, try customizing by swapping out regular milk in favor of almond or coconut milk for additional flavor options. Adding peppermint extract and drizzling caramel on top is possible if desired.

Java Chip Frappuccino

The Starbucks Java Chip Frappuccino is one of the tastiest, most energizing chocolate drinks you can order at Starbucks. It contains mocha sauce and chocolate cookie crumble topping blended with coffee, milk, ice, and rich whipped cream.The key ingredient in this delicious beverage is Java chips. It is different from traditional chocolate chips as they're made with a blend of semi-sweet dark chocolates combined with finely ground espresso beans for extra flavor.
This creates an incredibly rich yet smooth experience that gives Java Chip Frappuccino a unique taste. It's no wonder why this drink has become a Starbucks classic over time – its creamy texture, paired with subtle hints of espresso, makes it truly irresistible.

Double Chocolaty Chip Créme Frappuccino

From the name alone, the Double Chocolaty Chip Crème Frappuccino will surely be a hit with chocolate lovers worldwide. This decadent yet refreshing Frappuccino has become quite popular in recent years and can now be found on Starbucks' secret menu.The drink starts by blending creamy milk, crunchy chocolate chips, hazelnut syrup, and ice. It's then topped off with a swirl of whipped cream and more of those yummy chips.This fusion of chocolate flavors is unlike other drinks on their menu, making it a delightful beverage. Besides, it does not contain any coffee, making it an-all-age friendly drink that is also sweet yet healthy.And if customization is your thing – there are plenty of ways you could go about tweaking the recipe according to your unique tastes or dietary needs. For instance, adding almond or oat milk to get a creamier texture; using substitutions like mocha sauce instead or splashes of heavy cream; and even choosing different flavorings as well as spices like cinnamon if desired.These options make it easy for folks from all walks of life to mash up their favorite flavors, thereby giving them endless possibilities about how they prefer their Double Chocolate Chip Frappuccino served.

Hot Chocolate

Hot Chocolate is a popular café beverage that can be ordered at Starbucks. It comes in various flavors, including classic hot cocoa with steamed milk and Mocha made with espresso and hot chocolate sauce.The signature Café Mocha contains white or dark mocha syrup combined with espresso topped with rich cream. Another variation of the drink is the White Chocolate Mocha which has sweetened condensed milk as a base instead of heavy cream, making it lighter yet still creamy.To make it even more delicious, you can add whipped cream to your hot cocoas or try Iced White Chocolate Mocha - an icy blend of mocha flavor, cold foam, and caramel drizzle on top for extra sweetness. Those who prefer Frappuccinos can enjoy Java Chip Frappuccino - a combination of coffee and chunks of real solid Chocolate blended into lusciousness.Double Chocolaty Chip Crème Frappuccino features layers of java chips and a frothy cold brew with smooth white chocolate-flavored crème. They are all swirled together, producing rich chocolate flavor in every sip.

Iced Chocolate Almond Milk Shaken Espresso

The Iced Chocolate Almondmilk Shaken Espresso is a delicious addition to the list of 25 Best Chocolate Drinks to order at Starbucks. Combining sweet almond milk and bold espresso makes for a smooth and luxurious taste profile unlike any other.The key ingredients include shots of Starbucks' signature espresso, creamy almond milk, mocha flavor syrup, chocolate chips that melt as you drink, and ice cubes for an extra chill – all topped with irresistible whipped cream.It also features a benefits-filled 60-day returns policy that allows customers to return their beverages within two months after purchase if unsatisfied (offered by selected stores).

Reserve Dark Chocolate Mocha

Starbucks Reserve Dark Chocolate Mocha is a unique combination of single-origin coffee and handcrafted Chocolate. The flavor profile is unmistakably decadent, with notes of rich cocoa perfectly balanced by espresso and sweet cream.You can order special seasonal variations like the Smoked Butterscotch Reserve Dark Chocolate Mocha for an extra treat. It features dark chocolate mocha sauce overlaid with steamed smoked butterscotch-flavored milk.

White Chocolate Crème Frappuccino

The White Chocolate Crème Frappuccino from Starbucks is a rich and creamy blended beverage that's sure to satisfy your sweet cravings. It starts with espresso, white chocolate mocha sauce, and milk blended in perfect proportions creating a rich, syrupy base.Along with the sweetness comes real dairy whipped cream on top, creating a deliciously smooth drink bursting with flavor. This signature Frappuccino has 150mg of caffeine per Grand serving, so you get an energizing boost along with bold chocolatey goodness.The Grand version also adds 430 calories making it an ideal mid-day or evening treat. Like its mocha counterpart, variations like No Whip or Almond Milk can be added for those looking to customize their order or meet dietary needs.

Mocha Frappuccino

The Mocha Frappuccino, one of Starbucks' flagship beverages, is a classic coffee-based blended drink. It is made with espresso, semi-sweet chocolate chips, low-fat milk, ice, and flavored syrup.This popular treat was first introduced in 1994 as the Double Chocolatey Chip Frappuccino – but soon gained recognition as the 'Mocha Frappuccino' after its makeover in 2003 that incorporated real mocha sauce instead of chocolate ash.
In addition to the classic blend, you can enjoy its Iced variant, which provides a cool icy refreshment in summer. You can customize your order by adding a gingerbread flavor or top it off with whipped cream and crunchy pumpkin spice topping.

Reserve Iced Dark Chocolate Mocha

The Reserve Iced Dark Chocolate Mocha mixes Starbucks' signature single-origin coffees with dark, decadent, and deliciously rich chocolates. This cold beverage is made from brewed Starbucks Reserve espresso, which combines sweet chocolate flavors that blend perfectly with the deep and intense notes of the coffee.Its unique combination of these ingredients results in a truly luxurious drinking experience. Also, using single-origin beans adds complexity to the overall flavor since each region offers distinct characteristics, adding more depth to the beverage.Furthermore, this gourmet drink comes with small dollops of housemade vanilla whipped cream and cocoa powder for an extra touch of luxury and sweetness.

Peppermint Hot Chocolate

This unique twist on the classic hot Chocolate features a refreshing hint of peppermint that is perfect for warming up during cold winter days. Deliciously creamy and sweet, this popular Starbucks drink combines milk with a double shot of espresso and their signature flavored syrup or chocolate shavings to give it an extra kick.The addition of minty ingredients gives this hot beverage its distinct flavor and scent, making it a cozy, comforting treat.

Peppermint White Hot Chocolate

Peppermint White Hot Chocolate is a rich and creamy seasonal beverage from Starbucks. It is primarily made with white chocolate sauce, steamed milk, and some peppermint syrup. Furthermore, it is topped with light whipped cream and crunchy sugar bits for the special festive. The sweetness of this drink comes from the white Chocolate, which works perfectly against the subtle minty hint provided by the peppermint flavoring – making it an ideal treat to enjoy in winter.

This drink has gained immense popularity over time due to its luxurious taste and pleasant aroma that makes your pleasure buds dance in glee - so make sure you try one before they disappear for another year.

Tuxedo Mocha Frappuccino (Secret Menu)

The Tuxedo Mocha Frappuccino is a must-try for all the chocoholics out there. This popular, secret menu item was created to give everyone's favorite milk, and Mocha frap a rich, chocolatey twist.This drink features Starbucks' signature espresso blended with both their classic Mocha and white chocolate sauce. To top it off, this delicious beverage also includes a chocolate cookie crumble topping and whipped cream.If you're looking for ways how to order the Tuxedo Mocha Frappuccino like a pro and customize it according to your taste preferences, here is how to do it: Start by ordering regular Frappuccinos and double shots of classic Mocha and white chocolate sauce. And ask them to blend them together before pouring over whipping cream. Also, ensure they add some extra joy by finishing your masterpiece with their signature cookie crumble topping. If you prefer something sweet but not overly sugary – replace regular dairy cream with almond milk or coconut creamer.

Iced White Mocha With Sweet Cream Cold Foam & Caramel Drizzle (Secret Menu)

This luxurious and decadent Starbucks Secret Menu drink is a must-try for any chocoholic. It features saturated espresso, the sugary sweetness of the white mocha sauce, richly blended with sweet cream cold foam topped off with caramel drizzle.To order this heavenly treat from the hidden menu items list, start by requesting a Grande cold brew as your base and add a shot of espresso for that extra caffeine boost. You can customize it to your liking by adding pumps of white mocha sauce. Finally, finish it off with sweet cream cold foam and caramel drizzle.

Chocolate-Covered Strawberry Frappuccino (Secret Menu)

The Chocolate Covered Strawberry Frappuccino is another delicious addition to the list. A sweet combination of Frappuccino Roast Coffee, raspberry flavored syrup, and mocha sauce blended with java chips, strawberry puree, and ice make up this secret menu item.It has gained quite a cult following amongst Starbucks drinkers everywhere. This delicious treat provides all the tartness of fresh strawberries while being rounded out with smooth flavors of creamy Chocolate.

Caramel Cookie Bar Frappuccino (Secret Menu)

This delicious drink is a must-try for any chocolate lover. It features mocha sauce, a chocolate cookie crumble topping, and whipping cream to create that perfect combination of flavors.All these amazing ingredients are well-balanced. The sweet caramel taste perfectly compliments the bitterness of the dark cocoa used in this drink.

Ferrero Rocher Frappuccino (Secret Menu)

The Ferrero Rocher Frappuccino is delicious, combining the flavors of Chocolate and hazelnut. It is no wonder that this secret menu beverage has become popular with Starbucks customers and coffee enthusiasts around the world.
To make a Ferrero Rocher Frappuccino, you'll need to start off by ordering a Double Chocolate Chip Frappuccino as your base. Once you have this ready in your cup, add two mocha syrup pumps, one hazelnut syrup pump, and a splash of almond milk and blend it all.Customizing your own version of this sought-after Starbucks drink is possible and easy. You can substitute ingredients of your choice, like using a white chocolate sauce or adding more nutty flavorings.

Caramel And Nut Chocolate Bar Frappuccino (Secret Menu)

The Caramel and Nut Chocolate Bar Frappuccino is a delicious treat created using the classic combination of Chocolate, caramel, and nuts. It is made with Starbucks' signature mocha sauce blended with Frappuccino chips and then layered over their whipped cream blend.This secret menu item offers an experience of both sweet and crunchy tastes. You can customize your drink by adding in even more goodies like chocolate cookie crumbles topping.Add generous doses of sea salt-infused caramel syrup drizzles for more flavor complexity or almond milk for improved creaminess.

Kit Kat Frappuccino (Secret Menu)

The Kit Kat Frappuccino, a Starbucks secret menu item, is one of the best chocolate drinks you can order at the coffee chain. This delicious and creamy beverage start is a blend of mocha sauce, chocolate cookie crumble topping, and whipped cream for the perfect finish.Customers can customize their Kit Kat Frappuccino by adding syrups or extra espresso shots to make it even more flavorful. The Grande size of this sweet treat contains 480 calories and 95mg of caffeine, making it an ideal choice that should be savored in moderation.

Chocolate Covered Strawberry Cold Brew (Secret Menu)

The Chocolate Covered Strawberry Cold Brew is a delectable specialty coffee drink with an enticing taste and flavor.This secret menu item has a base of cold brew, with sweetened strawberry puree combined with steamed milk and mocha sauce. Top it off with velvety chocolate whipped cream and sugary syrup to further enhance its taste.The creamy texture produced by the combination of sweet ingredients leaves an incredible taste that is perfect for any coffee or chocolate enthusiast.

Frozen Hot Chocolate Frappuccino (Secret Menu)

The Frozen Hot Chocolate Frappuccino is a delicious custom cold treat from Starbucks' secret menu. It combines white and dark chocolate powder, espresso shots, steamed milk, and ice.Its frozen mix of coffee, cocoa, and cream gives a refreshing taste that is perfect to have on a hot day. Those wishing to customize their order can request ingredients of their choice.

Three C's Latte (Secret Menu)

The Three C's Latte is a delightful fusion of flavors created by Starbucks fans. It combines creamy caramel, smooth Chocolate, and zesty cinnamon to create a unique, delicious beverage sure to satisfy any sweet tooth.This secret menu item has become incredibly popular among coffee enthusiasts who appreciate its unique flavor combination. Here is how to order Three C's Latte:Start with a Grande Vanilla Bean Frappuccino before adding one pump each of Caramel Syrup and Cinnamon Dolce Syrup and two pumps of Mocha Sauce. Once this is all mixed together, you can mix in 1/2 cup of espresso shots for an extra energy kick.

Raspberry Mocha (Secret Menu)

The Raspberry Mocha is a velvety-smooth, secret menu item at Starbucks that packs an irresistible double punch of rich Chocolate and sweet fruity raspberry. The drink's base includes a double shot of espresso, then topped off with 2-5 pumps of mocha sauce, steamed milk, and creamy whipped cream.For an extra touch, it's typically finished with a sprinkling of chocolate cookie crumbs on top. When ordering the Raspberry Mocha, ask your barista for two or more shots with 2-5 pumps of mocha sauce and plenty of steamed milk or cold foam on top.

How To Order Chocolate Drinks At Starbucks Like A Pro

Getting the perfect chocolate drink at Starbucks is an art, and becoming familiar with their menu can be difficult. However, it does not have to stay that way. Armed with a few simple tips, you will soon be ordering like a pro.When placing an order for chocolate drinks at Starbucks, customize your selection to tailor it to your preferences. Feel free to ask for extra espresso shots if you want more energy throughout the day; add some flavored syrup, such as vanilla or caramel, if sweetness is desired.A popular combination among coffee lovers is adding mocha sauce and dark cocoa powder in hot beverages — offering customers an intense combination of taste and aroma like no other cocoa-based beverage offers. Additionally, feel free to specify what kind of milk should go in your coffee as well as how much foam - light/extra thick/super thick - you think works best on top of it all.

4 Ways To Add Chocolate To Your Starbucks Drink

Adding chocolate flavor to your Starbucks drinks can make them even tastier and more satisfying. From cocoa powder to syrups, here are five ways you can easily add chocolatey goodness to your favorite Starbucks creations.1. One way is by adding cocoa powder or hot chocolate mix directly into the drink. Simply scoop a tablespoon of either ingredient into your cup before ordering and enjoy an instant bump in sweetness with each sip (without adding too much sugar).2. You can also opt for flavored syrups such as hazelnut, dark Mocha, White Mocha, or even Irish cream – all of which will work perfectly with any coffee-based beverage.3. Chocolate toppings such as shavings and chips are also a popular choice; simply sprinkle some on top of your drink or use it in combination with other ingredients like whipped cream or seasonal fruit additions to create delicious sugary treats that taste just like dessert.4. Finally, if you prefer something without additional calories, try using extracts such as vanilla with a few drops of pure cocoa extract for added depth of flavor without excess sweetness.
Conclusion Starbucks chocolate drinks offer the ultimate delight for chocolate lovers. From creamy mochas to rich cold brews, these 25+ best Starbucks chocolate drinks boast a sumptuous sweetness and unparalleled flavor that can satisfy even your most intense cravings.Not only do they provide unique taste experiences, but thanks to the tips mentioned above, you order them like a pro. Even better with add-ons as it allows you to customize your favorite sweet beverage. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) What Is The Best Way To Add Chocolate To A Starbucks Drink? The best way to add Chocolate to a Starbucks drink is by using flavored syrups such as hazelnut, dark Mocha, White Mocha, or even Irish cream. Are There Secret Menu Drinks At Starbucks That Feature Chocolate? Yes. There are several secret menu drinks at Starbucks that offer unique combinations of flavors – including some featuring chocolate. Some popular examples include the Chocolate-Covered Strawberry Frappuccino and the Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Frappuccino. Does Starbucks Offer Chocolate-Flavored Coffees? Yes. Starbucks offers a variety of hot and cold coffee beverages that are infused with rich chocolate flavors. Some popular options include their Salted Caramel Mocha, White Chocolate Mocha, and Double Chocolatey Chip Frappuccino. Can I Customize My Starbucks Chocolate Drink? Absolutely. Depending on the type of beverage that you order, you can use a variety of add-ons, such as whipped cream, flavored syrups, and seasonal fruit, to customize your favorite Starbucks chocolate drinks. Can I Order Low-Calorie Chocolate Drinks From Starbucks? Yes. You can opt for lighter versions of some of your favorite chocolate beverages or use add-ons such as extracts and cocoa powder instead of syrups and sauces to help reduce the calorie count in your drink.
